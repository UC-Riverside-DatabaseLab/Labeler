About Us [view]
This site was built to help with the collaborative labeling and its goal is to provide a platform for labelers from around the world to share the labeling tasks. The users of the site are assigned tasks with posts and labels. Each task has a description and labelers have to choose labels for different posts of different tasks. Their work will be evaluated by an algorithm. The overall score of all labelings for a single task based on the posts in that task will be reported in the site. close
